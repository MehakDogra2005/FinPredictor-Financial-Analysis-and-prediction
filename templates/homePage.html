Our Value Stock Criteria

We evaluate these five key metrics to determine if a stock is truly undervalued

P/E Ratio

Price-to-Earnings ratio compared to industry and historical averages to assess valuation.

P/B Ratio

Price-to-Book value helps identify stocks trading below their intrinsic worth.

Return on Equity

Measures how effectively a company generates profits from shareholders' equity.

EBITDA Growth

1-year EBITDA growth rate indicates operational profitability trends.

Sales Growth

5-year compound annual sales growth shows long-term business expansion.
